# Google Ads Campaign for Iraaya Jewelry

## Project Overview

This capstone project focuses on designing a Google Ads campaign for Iraaya Jewelry, a handmade jewelry brand that offers earrings, bracelets, neck chains, and customized accessories.

The project aims to increase brand awareness, attract potential customers, and improve sales opportunities through targeted Google advertising.

---

## Objectives

* Increase brand awareness
* Generate customer inquiries
* Drive online traffic
* Improve sales opportunities
* Develop an effective digital advertising strategy

---

## Target Audience

### Demographics

* Age: 16–35 years
* Primarily female customers
* Students and working professionals

### Interests

* Fashion accessories
* Handmade products
* Personalized gifts
* Online shopping

---

## Campaign Planning

### Campaign Type

Google Search Campaign

### Campaign Goal

Website Traffic and Lead Generation

### Location Targeting

India

### Language

English

---

## Keyword Research

Target Keywords:

* handmade jewelry online
* customized jewelry gifts

* handmade bracelets
